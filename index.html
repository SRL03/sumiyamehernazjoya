About Me

Sumiya Mehernaz Joya

Founder & Designer, ArtHolic

As an artist myself, I always like to show the unique perspective of artists. Being a visual person, I have always found special resonance in design. The arts I create are a celebration of the colorful aspects of human life. My works are an exploration of culture and identity. I would like people to identify with my art in their own way. Developing my own techniques and aesthetics. I try to explore the relationship art and laws of nature and create a powerful emotional experience.
